About Us

Built on Relationships, Driven by Quality

We're a contract furniture specialist helping businesses across the UK create workspaces that inspire, educate and welcome.

Our Story

From Yorkshire to the Rest of the UK

Switch Contract Furniture was founded with a simple belief: that every workspace deserves furniture that's functional, beautiful and built to last. Based in Yorkshire, we've grown from a small local supplier into a trusted partner for businesses, schools and hospitality venues across England, Scotland and Wales.

We work closely with every client to understand their space, their people and their budget. Whether it's a single office refresh or a full building fit-out, we bring the same level of care, expertise and attention to detail to every project.
