Cookie Policy

Transparency about how we use cookies on our website

About This Policy

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and your choices regarding their use. If you have further questions about the cookies we use, please get in touch with us at hello@switchcontractfurniture.co.uk or call 0333 210 1309.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They allow websites to remember information about your visit, such as your preferences or login details. Cookies are widely used across the internet to improve user experience and website functionality.

Essential Cookies Only

Our website uses only essential cookies that are strictly necessary for the website to function properly. These essential cookies enable core functionality such as page navigation and access to secure areas of the website. Without these cookies, the website would not work as intended. Essential cookies do not require your consent under UK privacy law.

We do not currently use analytics cookies to track visitor behaviour, advertising cookies to display targeted advertisements, or any other non-essential cookies on our website.

What This Means for You

Because we only use essential cookies, you can browse our website without worrying about tracking or profiling. Your privacy is important to us, and we do not collect data about your browsing habits. You will not see targeted advertisements based on your activity on our site, and we do not use cookies to build detailed profiles of our visitors.

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your web browser settings. All modern browsers allow you to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent to your device. Please note that if you disable essential cookies, some parts of our website may not function properly. For more information on how to manage cookies in your browser, please visit the help section of your browser or www.aboutcookies.org.

Future Changes

As our website evolves, we may add analytics functionality to better understand how visitors use our site and improve our services. If we implement analytics cookies or any other non-essential cookies in the future, we will update this policy and seek your explicit consent before doing so. We will not add tracking or advertising cookies without your prior permission.

Third-Party Websites

Our website may contain links to other websites. This Cookie Policy applies only to our website. We are not responsible for the cookie practices of external websites. When you visit another website through a link from our site, please review their cookie policy to understand how they use cookies.
